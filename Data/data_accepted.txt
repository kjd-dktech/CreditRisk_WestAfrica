## Description of variables in Train/Test.csv

1. **Total_Amount**: The total loan amount initially disbursed to the customer.

2. **Total_Amount_to_Repay**: The total amount the customer is expected to repay, including principal, interest, and fees.

3. **loan_type_encoded**: The category or type of loan.

4. **disbursement_date**: The date when the loan amount was disbursed to the customer.

5. **duration**: The length of the loan term, typically expressed in days

13. **Lender_portion_Funded**: Percentage of the total loan amount funded by the lender.

15. **Lender_portion_to_be_repaid**: The portion of the outstanding loan that needs to be repaid to the lender.

16. **target**: This variables takes the value 0 or 1. 1 means the customer defaulted on the loan, whereas 0 means, the customer paid the loan.
